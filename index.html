About Saviour...

Traffic management on the road has become a severe problem of today's generation because of the population, urbanization and industrialization. There has been a tremendous growth in traffic, as a result emergency vehicles such as ambulance get stuck in traffic and waste their valuable time and golden hour. High priority must be given to human lives in the Ambulance which is travelling through the signal.

Our system using specialised software will identify and track the movement of the ambulance and will, give an updated route to reach its destination without having to stop at any traffic signal.
